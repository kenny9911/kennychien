The Agentic Hour is a podcast about enterprise AI in the agentic era, hosted by enterprise AI consultant Kenny Chien. Every other Tuesday, I sit down with an operator, researcher, or skeptic and push past the demo reel to what actually happens when agents meet production.

The show covers the same territory as my consulting and writing: agents in production, vibe coding under real constraints, agentic architecture and ontology, and forward deployed engineering (FDE). If you prefer the arguments in written form, start with the essays in Ideas.

"The best conversations about agents start where the demo ends. That is where the show starts too."

Frequently asked questions

What is The Agentic Hour about?

The Agentic Hour is a podcast about enterprise AI in the agentic era, hosted by enterprise AI consultant Kenny Chien. Each episode is a long-form conversation with an operator, researcher, or skeptic about agents in production — the architecture, the ontology, the failures, and the taste it takes to ship well.

How often do new episodes come out?

New episodes of The Agentic Hour are released every other Tuesday. Recent episodes run between 39 and 48 minutes — long enough for a real argument, short enough for a commute.

How can I be a guest on The Agentic Hour?

Email kenny.chien@gmail.com with a short pitch: who you are, what you have built or studied, and the one claim you are willing to defend for an hour. Practitioners with production stories get priority over vendors with product announcements.
